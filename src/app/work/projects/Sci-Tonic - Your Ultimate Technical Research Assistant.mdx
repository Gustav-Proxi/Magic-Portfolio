## Overview

Sci-Tonic is an AI-powered technical research assistant built to support researchers by offering instant access to critical research data, summarizing complex research papers, and extracting key information. By integrating NLP and machine learning models, Sci-Tonic accelerates the research process, helping researchers make data-driven decisions faster.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Summarization**: Utilized NLP models to extract and summarize essential information from lengthy research papers, articles, and journals, saving researchers time.
- **Contextual Search**: Integrated an intelligent search engine to help users find relevant research materials by understanding the context and keywords within their queries.
- **Key Insights Extraction**: Extracted key data points, facts, and findings from research articles to provide actionable insights to the users.
- **Cross-Referencing**: Enabled users to cross-reference citations and related research papers, facilitating comprehensive literature reviews.
- **Interactive Interface**: Developed a user-friendly interface where researchers can easily interact with the assistant, upload documents, and get results in real-time.

## Technologies Used

- **Python**: For implementing the backend logic, processing data, and interacting with machine learning models.
- **NLP Models (BERT, GPT-3)**: Used for summarizing documents and extracting meaningful information from research papers.
- **TensorFlow & Hugging Face**: Leveraged pre-trained models for natural language understanding and processing tasks.
- **Flask & Streamlit**: Built an interactive web interface to allow researchers to upload documents and get instant insights.
- **AWS**: Used for scalable cloud storage and compute resources for processing research data.

## Challenges and Learnings

One of the key challenges was ensuring that the summarization and extraction algorithms accurately preserved the technical depth of the research papers while keeping it concise. Fine-tuning language models to handle specialized technical vocabulary posed difficulties but provided valuable learning in adapting models for niche domains. Additionally, building a robust backend to handle the large volumes of research data efficiently was a significant hurdle.

## Outcome

Sci-Tonic streamlined the research process by providing researchers with quick, accurate summaries and key insights from technical research papers. With an **87% accuracy** in summarization and data extraction, Sci-Tonic has shown the potential to revolutionize how technical research is conducted. Researchers can now save valuable time while gaining deeper insights into the latest advancements in their fields.
